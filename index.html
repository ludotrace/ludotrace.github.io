LUDOTRACE

Your game.  Your data.

LudoTrace captures what actually happens when you play — and makes that data useful.

How it works

01 /
Install the mod

Drop it in your Data folder. No ESP, no MCM, no configuration. Recording starts the moment you load a save.

02 /
Play normally

Every session writes a JSONL log — kills, quests, locations, inventory changes, SPECIAL deltas. Automatic. Invisible.

03 /
Use the data

Paste it into an AI for coaching. Build a dashboard. Write a script. The log is plain text — it works with anything.
